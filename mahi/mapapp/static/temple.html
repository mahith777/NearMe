srilakshmi venkataramana temple

hosur

The Sri Lakshmi Venkataramana Swamy Temple is a beautiful hilltop temple near Hosur, located on a hill accessible by around 150-200 steps. It is situated on a small hill and is known for being a peaceful and scenic spot, often visited in the early morning or before dusk for the best views. The temple is protected by a grill maintained by the TVS group.
