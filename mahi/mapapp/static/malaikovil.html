Malai Kovil, also known as the Chandira Choodeshwarar Temple, is an ancient Shiva temple situated on a hill in Hosur, offering scenic views of the city. It is a popular tourist and pilgrimage site known for its architectural style, rich history, and panoramic views, especially at sunrise and sunset. The temple is accessible by road or a walking trail and is a significant historical landmark in the region
