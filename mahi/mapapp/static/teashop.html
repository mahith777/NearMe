Cam Soori Tea Shop is a tea shop in Hosur, Tamil Nadu, known for serving tea, beverages, and snacks. It has a cozy ambiance and offers a menu that includes both casual and more indulgent dining experiences. The shop has multiple locations, including one in Alasanatham, Sanasandiram, and another possibly in Ganga Nagar. There is also a new brand, "Cam Soori Tea Kadai," which celebrated its grand opening on November 3, 2025
